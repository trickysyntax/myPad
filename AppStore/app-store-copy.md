# myPad Studio — App Store Copy

## Promotional Text

Organize every selection, vendor, and room in one place. Browse 1,400+ vendors and 9,000+ product templates. Track budgets, statuses, and finishes across projects.

---

## Description

myPad Studio is the project management platform built specifically for interior designers. From first client meeting to final installation, myPad keeps every vendor relationship, product selection, fabric finish, and budget line organized in one native Apple app.

DESIGNED FOR THE WAY DESIGNERS WORK

Organize projects by room. Track each selection through its full lifecycle — proposed, approved, ordered, delivered, installed. Compare candidates side by side. Capture on-site photos directly into any selection or room. See your budget at a glance, broken down by room and category, with real-time variance tracking against your target.

YOUR VENDOR DIRECTORY, ELEVATED

Browse a curated library of over 1,400 industry vendors — furniture makers, textile houses, lighting studios, and more. Each vendor profile includes contact details, trade pricing tiers, sourcing notes, and a full product catalog. Search by name, category, or tag to find the right source fast.

A LIVING PRODUCT CATALOG

Access over 9,000 reusable asset templates linked directly to their vendors. Every asset carries its specifications, dimensions, lead times, pricing, and an unlimited library of finishes and fabric options. Add a chair to one project today, and it's ready to drop into the next project tomorrow — no re-entering data.

FINISHES WITHOUT LIMITS

Track every fabric, colorway, and finish as a first-class record — not a spreadsheet column. Each finish carries its own vendor, grade, yardage, pricing, stock status, and swatch image. Compare finishes across selections. Pull a finish from one room into another. MyPad handles the details so nothing gets lost between the sample room and the job site.

3D SPACE CAPTURE

On iPad and iPhone with LiDAR, capture a 3D scan of any room or full project directly inside myPad. View your spatial captures in an interactive 3D viewer, attach them to rooms, and give your clients a walkthrough of the space before a single piece of furniture arrives.

BUDGET AT A GLANCE

Set a project budget. Track spending by room and category. See totals update in real time as selections move from proposed to ordered. Export budget summaries for client presentations.

PHOTO-RICH WORKFLOW

Capture photos on-site or pull from your library. Attach them to rooms, selections, or individual finishes. Swipe through full-screen galleries with pinch-to-zoom. Every image is synced to the server and available across your devices.

SYNC ACROSS DEVICES

myPad Studio syncs your projects, vendors, selections, and photos to a secure server. Work on your iPad at the job site, review on your Mac at the studio, and check in on your iPhone between meetings. Offline changes sync automatically when you reconnect.

BUILT FOR THE APPLE ECOSYSTEM

Native SwiftUI app optimized for iPad, iPhone, and Mac. Warm ivory interface with gold accents and editorial serif typography — designed to feel like a professional tool, not a generic productivity app. Supports iPadOS multitasking, keyboard shortcuts on Mac, and system-wide search.

WHO IT'S FOR

myPad Studio is built for interior designers, design firms, and home stagers who manage multiple projects, vendor relationships, and product selections. Whether you're a solo designer or part of a team, myPad replaces the spreadsheets, shared drives, and sticky notes with a single, reliable system.
